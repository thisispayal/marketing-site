Who uses ImageShare?
A breakdown of our user demographics

Users receive second opinions frequently from an undefined number of senders and therefore need an image exchange service that is different from one that serves a radiology-focused user. Cardiology and Radiology workflows are fundamentally different and require fundamentally different services to meet each of their respective needs. We offer a complete service offering and business model that reflects the way specialized practices receive second opinions and transfers. Building an open network requires hands-on training and immediately available top level support.
