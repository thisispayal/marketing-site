Overview

No Setup Sending

Receive full DICOM imaging studies from anyone in the world without installing any software. ImageShare is entirely web-based, meaning all you need is a modern web-browser — such as IE, Chrome, or Safari. There's no software to install or hardware to purchase.

Access Anytime

Available 24 hours a day, ImageShare works on any Internet-connected device. Being web-based means you simply need an updated browser and Internet connection. Hospital workflows, personal laptops, and mobile devices can all access ImageShare.

Share with Colleagues

Collaborate with anyone. Share images with colleagues at your facility or create accounts to send images to outside providers. Patients see many providers and ImageShare lets you coordinate with any of them regardless of where they are in the world.

View Images Online

View DICOM images online using either our mobile-friendly viewer or FDA-approved browser-based viewer. Our full featuer browser viewer includes measurement, window-level, wiling, and other tools. Cardiac caths, CTs, echos, and MRIs are all viewable online.

Uses

ImageShare connects high acuity providers with those that rely on their expertise. No setup uploading allows anyone to send images with no software to install or accounts to create. Our secure portals helps providers from around the world to communicate and deliver better patient care.

Referrals

Receive a patient's images prior to their arrival at your hospital. 24-hour availability makes it easier for providers in your network to reach you and send you patients.

Quality Assurance

Easily conduct quality reviews by using ImagaeShare to collect cases and distribute them to reviewers. Then use ImageShare to view the case and complete reports.

Clinical Trials

Collect case images from research sites and distribute them to your proctors or core lab. Exams can be anonymized and organized under a single patient identifer.

Features

Accessible Anywhere
Use ImageShare from any web-enabled device, including mobile devices. Home computers, hospital workstations, tablets, and smartphones can all access ImageShare anytime.

ImageShare has undergone HIPAA and security audits conducted by a third-party organization. Documentation for our audit can be found here and our security practices can be accessed here.

ImageShare users needing help can call our support line, which is posted on every page of our application. All support calls connect to either an engineer or product specialist.
View in Browser
Cine playback, window/level measurement, and other standard viewing tools are available in our online browser-based DICOM viewers.
Download
Download full copies of your images to your computer. Sutides can be downloadable in .zip format or as a virtual CD (.iso).
PACS
Upload images to ImageShare directly from your PACS. ImageShare PACS Endpoint software installs at your center and routes images from your systems to ImageShare.
File Type Neutral
ImageShare allows you to upload and receive files of any type, including DICOM, .avi, .jpg, .doc Word documents, and .ppt PowerPoint files.
